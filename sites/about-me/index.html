About Me

I’m a European Salesforce MVP and Marketing Automation Architect focused on implementing Marketing Automation and AI Agents in Salesforce Ecosystem.

I worked on the local, regional, and global teams as an internal leader and external consultant. This experience gives me multiple perspectives on every marketing challenge.

Having worked in digital marketing, e-commerce, sales, and editorial, I know how good automation implementation ties in multiple departments of the company and requires sincere cooperation of the teams.

My passion is business process automation. Good asset and campaign templates. Data integration & architecture. Agents and custom developments. All those elements are necessary to leverage the full potential of modern platforms. And bring the most value and highest ROI to the company.
